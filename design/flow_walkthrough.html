How the AL RAWDA ERP Works

The app screens, marked step-by-step — focusing on creating receipts, multi-selecting them, requesting approval, and the manager approving the combined receipt.

AL RAWDA GROUP HAJJ & UMRAH SERVICES — Doha, Qatar
Prototype walkthrough · v1.0 · 09 June 2026

The flow at a glance

A receipt travels from the counter staff to the manager. The numbered steps below are marked on the screens that follow.
1Login→ 2Create invoice→ 3Create receipt→ 4Select receipts→ 5Request approval→ 6Manager reviews combined→ 7Approve → posted

Who does what

3
Counter staff create receipts against a customer's invoice (one receipt can cover several packages).
4
Counter staff tick the day's receipts in the list (multi-select) — the combined total is shown.
5
Counter staff press Request approval — the ticked receipts become one combined request sent to the manager.
6
Manager opens Receipt Approval and sees the combined request: all receipts grouped with one total.
7
Manager approves — the receipts lock and count as income (or rejects, sending them back to draft).
