A payment bundles the booked receipts. The manager verifies the total against the receipts and approves — which locks the payment and its receipts. Reject sends them back to Open. Separation of duties: staff book, manager approves.

Document numbering — dynamic prefix

Build a numbering format from tokens, then choose a reset cycle. With a yearly cycle a new prefix block is created each year and the sequence restarts from 1 automatically — so every year can carry its own model.
INV-{YYYY}-{0000}
+ {PREFIX}+ {YYYY}+ {YY}+ {MM}+ {SEQ}+ text
