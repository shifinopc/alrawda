Payment Approval — Manager Check the payment and the receipts inside it, then approve
A payment bundles the booked receipts. The manager verifies the payment total against the receipts and approves — which locks the payment and its receipts (they can no longer be edited). Reject sends the receipts back to Open. Separation of duties: staff book, manager approves.
